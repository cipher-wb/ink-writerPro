- Templates: ink-writer/templates/output/大纲-卷节拍表.md, 大纲-总纲.md
- References: ink-writer/references/shared/ (core-constraints, cool-points-guide, golden-opening-patterns, strand-weave-pattern)
- Genre profiles: ink-writer/references/genre-profiles.md
- Step flow: Step 0→0.2→0.3→0.7→0.8→1→2A→2A.1→2A.5→2B→2C→3→4→4.5→5→5.5→6
- writer-agent touch points for new iron law: 铁律定义, MCC自检表, 失败处理策略, selfcheck JSON示例, 禁止事项, 硬性指标计数, 成功标准

- high-point-checker SELLING_POINT rules: SELLING_POINT_DEFICIT(critical: 大卖点缺失/小卖点<2; high: 无反应段; medium: 无情绪触发) + SELLING_POINT_FRONT_LOADING(high: 前1/3无小卖点)
- step-3-review-gate: 卖点密度门禁在逻辑/大纲合规门禁之后、回退机制之前
- rolling window: 连续2章大卖点缺失→critical(原为3章连续零爽点→high)
- proofreading-checker layers: L1修辞重复, L2段落结构, L3代词清晰度, L4文化/时代错位, L5跨章风格一致性
- writer-agent iron laws: L1数字即承诺, L2动作即物理, L3属性即锁定, L4空间即连续, L5大纲即合同, L6否定即禁区, L7卖点落地律, L8主角能动律
- MCC self-check: 9项（原8项+主角能动性），self-consistency scan: 6项（原5项+SC-6主角能动性检查）

- ink-plan Step 6 chapter format: `爽点配方` → `大卖点类型`（新增11种合法类型），新增 `大卖点描述`（≤80字）和 `小卖点`（2个×≤40字）
- ink-plan Step 5 volume skeleton: `爽点密度规划` 表升级为 `卖点密度规划` 含大小卖点列
- ink-plan Step 8 validation: 4 条新 hard fail（大卖点缺失/小卖点<2/前3章类型重复/非法类型）
- 节拍表模板: Section 7 新增卖点密度硬约束说明 + 爽点链表新增 `大卖点类型` 列
- golden_three.py ch1 contract: `ch1_closure_contract` 字段含 ability_display/crisis/ability_benefit + validation_rules
- ink-plan Step 6 黄金三章附加规则: 第1章闭环硬约束（能力展示→危机→能力收益）
- ink-plan Step 8 validation 1.7: 第1章完整小闭环检查（4条 hard fail）
- golden_three_threshold 默认值出现在 3 处：golden-three-checker.md / ink-review/SKILL.md / ink-write/SKILL.md（引用）
- audit_mode 三级：normal（默认=原strict）/ strict（hard block）/ relaxed（旧标准降级），读取自 state.json
- ink-review/SKILL.md 也引用 golden_three_threshold 阈值，修改阈值时需同步
- golden-three-checker CH1 闭环规则: CH1_NO_ABILITY_BENEFIT(critical) / CH1_PASSIVE_PROTAGONIST(critical) / CH1_ABSTRACT_PAYOFF(high) / CH1_LATE_CRISIS(high)
